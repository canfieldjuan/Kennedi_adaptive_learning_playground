# v0.3.57 Bear Cafe Pickup-Window Production-Art Proof

One-scene visual proof under `art-production-assets.contract.md`. This slice
does not roll the style across Bear Cafe and does not claim owner look approval.
Package version assignment is deferred until an approved proof is integrated;
the proof branch does not consume a release number merely for review.

## Before Code

### Root Cause

The Bear Cafe delivery stage is playable and illustrated, but its visual
composition remains a sparse arrangement of a small dynamic tray and a small
customer window over the generic cafe counter. It does not yet prove the richer
production-art workflow, editable source, environmental density, character
scale, responsive crop, or ownership record required before a broad graphics
rollout.

The cause is not missing gameplay. The stage has no production-editor source or
dedicated local environment export; its scene framing is assembled from generic
background SVG plus CSS around the dynamic customer and order.

### Art Direction Standard

- Preserve the established purple ink and warm flat-fill language while adding
  a clear cafe pickup identity: large framed service opening, scalloped awning,
  tile/wood structure, shelf and kitchen silhouettes, and intentional empty
  zones for the live bear and tray.
- The active order, live character, and Deliver control remain the strongest
  visual elements. Background contrast stays lower and no decorative object
  resembles a selectable food or count target.
- Desktop and phone crops retain the pickup opening, character, order, and
  action. Minor edge props may crop; interactive objects may not shrink for art.

### Source and Ownership Standard

- Tool: Inkscape, a local vector-design application; record the exact installed
  version in provenance before export.
- Origin: Category A original project artwork made from basic shapes and
  paths; no imported third-party or project legacy art is embedded in the source.
- AI assistance: none.
- Editable source: committed Inkscape SVG; production export: separately
  optimized standalone local SVG without unnecessary editor metadata.
- Status at PR open: `rights_verified`, with owner look approval pending. Do not
  mark `look_approved` or `production_ready` before the owner approves the proof.

### Verification Plan

- Inspect the source in Inkscape and independently render the production SVG.
- Validate SVG XML, `viewBox`, local-only references, absence of text/fonts,
  reasonable path/object count, and transparent/dynamic-content zones.
- Run focused Bear Cafe environment/runtime tests, full `npm test`, typecheck,
  build, viewport tests, lint if present, work-contract check, and diff check.
- Browser-smoke the success path through delivery at desktop and 390x844;
  verify visual crop, non-overlap, console state, single completion event, and
  unchanged event metadata.
- Generate proposed desktop/mobile screenshots and a before/proposed contact
  sheet, then stop for owner look approval before any broader Bear Cafe art.
