# v0.3.36 Words — Pip's Word Workshop (visual arc stage 3)

Third deliverable of the visual enrichment arc (issue #55): the three Words
modes get one shared illustrated environment. Presentation only. Traces to
`docs/visual-environment-audit.md` (Words section) under
`docs/contracts/game-environment.contract.md`.

## Before Code

### Root Cause (slice)

All three Words modes (initial-sound matcher, blending, word builder) float
Pip and the illustrated cards on the dark navy app background — warm subjects,
zero place. The sound-out chips hang in space with no rail, the builder's
slots/tiles sit on nothing, and Pip hovers in a plain gray circle rather than
guiding from a room.

### Correct Fix Must Touch

- `src/modules/phonics-match/workshop-environment.ts` (new) — the game-owned
  **Word Workshop** scene: soft warm wall, a window with simple outdoor
  shapes, a low bookshelf with plain colored book spines (**no letters — a
  hard guardrail: nothing in the scene may read as a letter, tile, or
  choice**), a pin-board shape, a plant/pencil-jar prop, and a floor/rug band.
  `createWorkshopEnvironment()` returns the aria-hidden,
  `pointer-events: none` layer. Render-verified.
- `src/modules/phonics-match/PhonicsMatchActivity.ts` and
  `WordBuilderActivity.ts` — container gains the `phonics-workshop` class and
  mounts the environment first-child (both runtimes = all three modes; the
  matcher serves initial sounds and blending). No handler/event/hint changes.
- `src/styles/child-ui.css` —
  - `.phonics-workshop` becomes a warm illustrated surface (light wall tone
    override, positioning context, content stacking above the inert layer —
    the Bear Cafe pattern);
  - scoped ink-color overrides for title/prompt/feedback (the shared white
    text is for the dark background);
  - warm card treatment for the choice grid inside the workshop (cream/ink
    instead of dark navy cards) with the **display-board framing** behind the
    card grid; the sound-out strip gets a **rail** underline (blending); the
    builder's slots sit on a **table-board** panel; Pip's circle becomes a
    warm workshop spot;
  - mobile: board/rail chrome stays shallow, minor scene props hidden,
    tap-target sizes untouched;
  - any new decorative motion: none (Pip's existing guarded animations are
    the motion budget).
- Tests — `tests/modules/phonics-match.test.ts` / `word-builder.test.ts`:
  the workshop layer renders once per mode, aria-hidden, and the scene SVG
  contains no `<text>` and no letter glyph content; existing behavioral
  suites stay green (events, hints, chains). `mobile-child-ui.test.ts`:
  the workshop layer is `pointer-events: none` and hides minor props on
  phones.
- `docs/captures/word-workshop/` (new) — matcher, blending, builder desktop
  + matcher at ~390×844, WebP, for owner look approval.
- `package.json` / `package-lock.json` — version bump to `0.3.36`.
- Docs note in `MVP_BASELINE.md`.

### Explicit Non-Scope / Must Not Change

- Game rules, chains, per-skill evidence, hint semantics, Pip's behavior and
  mouth logic, event emission — untouched.
- No decorative letters, letter blocks, or readable words anywhere in the
  scene; no new animation; no changes to other games or the shared dark
  background used elsewhere.
- The word cards' own art (arc-complete set) is untouched.

## Contract Amendments

- **Pin-board repositioned + utility buttons ink-tinted after live capture
  review.** The centered pin-board prop collided with the activity title on
  wide viewports (moved left of center), and the shared translucent-white
  Home/Repeat buttons washed out on the warm wall (scoped ink tint added).
  Both caught by looking, not by tests.
