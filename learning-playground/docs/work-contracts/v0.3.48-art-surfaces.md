# v0.3.48 Bear Art Studio slice 4 — stage poster and wall picture

Fourth art slice: the spec's two remaining art surfaces. The stage poster
serves the performing/role-play loves; the wall picture is art *for the
bear house* — the fantasy that her pictures hang somewhere.

## Before Code

### Root Cause (slice)

The spec's "first art surfaces" list named five surfaces; two are still
unbuilt (stage poster, bear house wall picture). Free decorating currently
has only the plain card and the shirt — no performing surface, no
make-something-for-the-house surface, and the surface machinery added in
slice 2 (`surface: 'shirt'`) supports exactly this kind of growth.

### Required Change Surface

- `src/modules/bear-art-studio/studio-art.ts` — `posterSurfaceSvg(fill)`
  (portrait poster with a curtain header — performing) and
  `wallFrameSurfaceSvg(fill)` (landscape canvas in a wood frame — the
  bear-house wall). Both take the paintable fill like the shirt; both
  render-checked; no `<text>`, no emoji, flat SVG (no nested transforms).
- `bear-art-studio.types.ts` — `surface: 'card' | 'shirt' | 'poster' |
  'wall_frame'` on free-decorate content.
- `BearArtStudioActivity.ts` — the shirt-specific backdrop logic
  generalizes to a surface-backdrop map (card = tinted card background;
  shirt/poster/wall_frame = repaintable backdrop SVG). No mode, event, or
  rule changes; `art_surface_id` metadata carries the new surfaces so the
  gallery derivation works unchanged.
- `src/content/activities/` — `art-studio-stage-poster.json` (Mama Bear:
  "Make a poster for tonight's show!", star-forward sticker set, 4 slots)
  and `art-studio-wall-picture.json` (Daddy Bear: "Make a picture for the
  bear house wall.", full sticker set, 6 slots). Both art / color_fill /
  free_decorate, difficulty inside the L0/L1 bands, weak transfer
  (`same_format_new_examples`) with distinct context ids — no briefs or
  detectors needed.
- Chain rework (finale stays dress-up): story → **poster** → **wall
  picture** → dress. `art-studio-story-outside.json` bumps to version 2
  (next pointer only).
- `activity-catalog.ts` + `curriculum.v1.json` (data only:
  `color_fill.activity_contexts` += 2).
- `child-ui.css` — poster (portrait, 2×2 slots) and wall-frame (landscape,
  3×2 slots) card variants + phone compaction.
- Tests — `bear-art-studio.test.ts`: both surfaces render their backdrop,
  swatch taps repaint it, completion carries the new `art_surface_id`s;
  chain-integrity test updated. Viewport suite run locally.
- `docs/captures/bear-art-studio/` — poster + wall desktop, poster mobile.
- `package.json` / `package-lock.json` — `0.3.48`; `MVP_BASELINE.md` note.
