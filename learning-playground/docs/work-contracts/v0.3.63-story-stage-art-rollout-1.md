# v0.3.63 Story Stage Production-Art Rollout Slice 1

One bounded post-approval rollout under
`docs/contracts/art-production-assets.contract.md`: complete the production-art
journey for Princess Poppy's Lost Friend story in the Enchanted Forest. This is
not a full Story Stage art migration.

## Before Code

### Root Cause

The owner-approved Story Stage production style currently appears only for the
exact `lost-log` + Poppy + forest scene. The six other unique scene beats in that
same child-selected Lost Friend journey still render geometry assembled inside
`src/modules/story-stage/story-art.ts`. A child moving through one story therefore
crosses between the approved production environment and the older in-code visual
system, while those six scenes still lack separately editable Inkscape sources,
local production exports, and family-level provenance.

The cause is not story resolution, narration, branching, or responsive layout.
PR #120 intentionally proved one exact scene before rollout; its narrow
three-condition runtime guard now exposes the approved asset for only one of the
seven unique Lost Friend art keys.

### Correct Fix Must Touch

- `design-source/story-stage/lost-friend-poppy-forest/` (new): six layered,
  editable Inkscape SVG sources for `lost-intro`, `lost-problem`, `lost-where`,
  `lost-bush`, `lost-help`, and `lost-ending`. The merged
  `story-stage-lost-log-proof.svg` remains the seventh scene source.
- `public/assets/images/` (new exports): six optimized, local 400x250 SVGs with
  stable names matching those scene beats. The merged
  `story-stage-lost-log-proof.svg` remains the seventh production export.
- `src/modules/story-stage/story-art.ts`: replace the one-off proof guard with a
  closed local asset map for all seven Lost Friend art keys, eligible only when
  `characterArt === "poppy"` and `settingArt === "forest"`. Every unmatched
  scene, character, setting, and unknown key must retain the existing dynamic
  builders and fallback behavior.
- `tests/modules/story-stage.test.ts`: prove all seven exact-combination scene
  keys resolve to their intended local decorative assets; prove alternate
  character, setting, story family, and unknown-key paths remain dynamic; retain
  the no-event, non-interactive, and `aria-hidden` boundaries.
- `docs/art/asset-provenance.md`: add the cohesive rollout family, every source
  and export path, Category A rights, approved-direction inheritance, current
  review status, and complete no-third-party/no-AI declarations.
- `docs/captures/story-stage/`: add one desktop journey capture, one mobile
  journey capture, and a contact sheet showing all seven production scenes.
- This work contract: record implementation, verification, and a line-cited cold
  diff reconstruction before publication.

### Art Direction Standard

- Preserve the approved Lost Log proof's original purple ink, warm flat fills,
  rounded silhouettes, layered forest depth, pale winding path, gentle light,
  large readable characters, and foreground framing.
- Each scene must communicate its authored beat before narration: joyful
  companionship, safe paw-print search, path-or-owl decision, visible tail in a
  giggling bush, owl/log discovery, gentle courage choice, and safe reunion.
- Maintain continuity across all seven scenes: the same Poppy construction,
  Biscuit construction, forest palette, scale language, and left-to-right visual
  storytelling. Preserve the exact approved Lost Log asset without redrawing it.
- Keep decisive clues in the mobile-safe central field. Decorative edge foliage
  may crop, but Poppy, Biscuit, owl, paw prints, path, bush, bubbles/song motif,
  and reunion must remain legible in the existing 400x250 stage.
- Art remains decorative and non-interactive. Semantic narration and controls
  remain the accessible source of instructions.

### Source and Ownership Standard

- Tool: Inkscape 1.4.4 Flatpak (`org.inkscape.Inkscape`).
- Origin: Category A original project artwork built only from basic shapes and
  paths. The merged project-owned Lost Log proof supplies the approved visual
  construction standard; no third-party or generated geometry is incorporated.
- AI assistance: none.
- Each new scene receives a layered editable Inkscape SVG and a separate plain
  local SVG export with unnecessary editor metadata removed.
- The complete third-party resource list is `None`; no unlisted stock, library,
  plugin, font, texture, or generated element may remain.
- The proof direction is `look_approved`. New rollout assets remain
  `rights_verified` until the owner reviews this scene family; approval of style
  direction is not falsely reported as approval of every new composition.

  approval for this separately contracted rollout direction. The completed
  family still requires visual owner review before `look_approved` status.
