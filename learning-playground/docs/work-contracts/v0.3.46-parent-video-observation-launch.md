# v0.3.46 Parent Video Observation Launch

Make the already-approved local video/response pair available for the real-world
observation required by the roadmap without expanding content or child-home
scope.

## Before Code

  vocabulary breadth or delayed review content at
  `CURRENT_STATUS_AND_ROADMAP.md:334-337`.

### Root Cause

The first local video and separate response activity are implemented, approved,
and honestly logged, but they are discoverable only by typing a direct activity
hash. The roadmap requires observing this pair before expanding vocabulary
content, while the product gives the parent no normal way to offer it. The
missing capability is parent-controlled access to existing content, not more
content or different evidence rules.

### Correct Fix Must Touch

- Extend `docs/contracts/video-vault-evidence.contract.md` with a parent
  observation-access rule: a parent launch may route to the existing local
  vault, emits no evidence itself, and does not add Video to the child home.
- Update `src/modules/parent-panel/ParentPanel.ts` to render a second compact
  parent-started card for the existing `video-vault` activity.
- The card must identify the local `Bear Bakes Bread` clip, explain that the
  clip and question are separate evidence steps, and route only after the
  parent presses its launch button.
- Honor the existing `video_enabled` parent setting: when playback is Off, the
  card remains explanatory but its launch button cannot navigate.
- Preserve the existing Bear Cafe launch behavior while sharing only the small
  card-construction code needed to render two entries.
- Update `tests/contract/parent-game-launch.test.ts` to prove both parent launch
  routes, parent ownership, and the unchanged four-slot child home with no
  Videos card.
- Advance package, baseline, roadmap, and verification state to v0.3.46.
