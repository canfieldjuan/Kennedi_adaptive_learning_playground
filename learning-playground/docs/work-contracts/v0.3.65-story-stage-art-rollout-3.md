# v0.3.65 Story Stage Production-Art Rollout Slice 3

One bounded post-approval rollout under
`docs/contracts/art-production-assets.contract.md`: complete the production-art
journey for Princess Poppy's Lost Friend story in the Cozy Town. This is not a
full Story Stage art migration.

## Before Code

### Root Cause

The merged production-art maps cover Princess Poppy's Lost Friend and Special
Delivery journeys only when the selected setting is the Enchanted Forest.
Lost Friend also supports Cozy Town, but all seven scene keys for the exact
Poppy + Cozy Town selection still render geometry assembled inside
`src/modules/story-stage/story-art.ts`. The child's selected setting therefore
does not yet produce a separately editable, locally exported Cozy Town journey
with asset-level provenance and responsive visual evidence.

The cause is not story selection, resolution, narration, branching, history,
speech, persistence, or layout. The runtime has no closed production-asset map
for the exact Poppy + Cozy Town + Lost Friend context, and the corresponding
seven editable sources and exports do not exist.

### Art Direction Standard

- Preserve the approved Story Stage purple ink, warm flat fills, rounded
  silhouettes, large readable characters, layered depth, and calm visual
  density while making Cozy Town visibly distinct from the forest.
- Establish one consistent Cozy Town environment across all seven scenes:
  warm twinkling windows, rounded storefronts/homes, a pale cobbled lane,
  flower boxes, soft rooflines, and a central town-square or gate landmark.
- Preserve the approved Poppy and Biscuit construction exactly enough to read
  as the same characters across settings. Adapt the owl, paw prints, clue,
  bubbles/song motif, and reunion props to the town without changing the
  authored beats.
- Each scene must communicate its beat before narration: welcoming arrival,
  safe missing-friend clue, two routes, giggling hedge, owl/gate discovery,
  gentle help choice, and safe reunion.
- Keep decisive clues inside the mobile-safe center. Edge buildings and flower
  boxes may crop, but Poppy, Biscuit, owl, paw prints, route split, clue object,
  help motif, and reunion must remain legible in the existing 400x250 stage.
- Art remains decorative and non-interactive. Semantic narration and controls
  remain the accessible source of instructions.

  The seven new Cozy Town compositions remain `rights_verified` until the owner
  reviews this family; prior approval must not be reported as approval of these
