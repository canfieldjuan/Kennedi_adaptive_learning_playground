# Reward Contract

## Allowed rewards
- Short animation
- Gentle sound
- Spoken encouragement
- Unlocking a creative sticker after session completion
- Parent-visible milestone
- "You figured it out" feedback
- Curiosity prompt after success

## Forbidden rewards
- Streaks
- Daily pressure
- Random loot boxes
- Infinite levels
- Punitive loss states
- Social comparison
- Leaderboards
- Ads
- Engagement bait

## Reward language
Prefer:
- "You found it."
- "You solved it."
- "You tried again."
- "That was a tricky one."
- "You noticed the pattern."

Avoid:
- "You are so smart."
- "You failed."
- "Wrong."
- "Try harder."
- "You lost."
